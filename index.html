Process Unit Configuration — Deep Conversion Refinery
CDU
Crude Distillation Unit
Primary atmospheric distillation of 110,000 bpd Hassi Messaoud crude. Separates into naphtha, kerosene, gas oil, and residue fractions.
VDU
Vacuum Distillation Unit
Processes atmospheric residue under vacuum to extract vacuum gas oil (VGO) and vacuum residue for downstream conversion.
HCU
Hydrocracker Unit
Key "deep conversion" unit. Converts heavy VGO into diesel and naphtha using hydrogen under high pressure. Maximises distillate yield.
HDS
Gas Oil Hydrodesulfurizer
Removes sulphur from gas oil streams to meet Euro-standard diesel specifications (≤10 ppm S).
NHT
Naphtha Hydrotreating
Pre-treats naphtha before catalytic reforming to remove sulphur, nitrogen, and metals that would poison the reformer catalyst.
CCR
Continuous Catalytic Reformer
Converts hydrotreated naphtha into high-octane reformate for gasoline blending and produces hydrogen as a by-product for HCU/HDS.
ISOM
Isomerization Unit
Converts light straight-run naphtha (C5/C6) into higher-octane isomerate for gasoline blending pool.
SDA
Solvent Deasphalting
Extracts deasphalted oil (DAO) from vacuum residue using propane/butane solvent. Enables deep conversion of heavy residue.
ARU
Amine Recovery Unit
Recovers H₂S from sour gas streams across the refinery using amine absorption. Feeds sulphur recovery.
SRU
Sulphur Recovery Unit
Converts H₂S from ARU into elemental sulphur (Claus process). Required for environmental compliance and export.
WWTP
Wastewater Treatment Plant
Treats process wastewater and cooling water blowdown to meet Algerian environmental discharge standards.
